Meet the Photographers

Michelle and Cassandra are the photographers and cofounders behind InDetail Photography. They are passionate about capturing you and your loved ones' uniqueness in a keepsake that will last a lifetime. They strive to create a comfortable and safe environment in which to capture both your personality and beauty.

Michelle

Michelle is a passionate mother of three with experience both behind the lens and assisting other professional photographers. She decided to take the reigns in her own photography business in 2022 and InDetail Photography was born.

Cassandra

Cassandra is a hobbyist turned professional photographer. She has extensive background in child care management with an abundance of time working with young children and their parents. Taken in combination with her experience behind the lens and in Adobe Lightroom, she has the experience to find and capture you and your loved ones' best side.
